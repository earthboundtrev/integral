# Holistic development model — what Integral tracks

Integral is built to tend **development across the whole person** — not one lane (mood, habits, fitness, or finance) but honest daily check-ins across every domain that shapes a life.

Last updated: July 2026.

**Related:** [INPUT_AND_CONSUMPTION_COMPARISON.md](INPUT_AND_CONSUMPTION_COMPARISON.md) (food, art, reading, content vs dedicated apps) · [COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE.md](COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE.md) (life OS, journal, fitness competitors)

---

## Design principles

| Principle | What it means in the app |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Whole person** | Financial, physical, mental, emotional, spiritual, relational, cultural, and environmental life — plus inputs (what you consume). |
| **Low friction** | Rating + Save is enough. No category is mandatory every day. |
| **Honest logging** | Doomscrolling, bad work weeks, and low mood are valid data. |
| **Cross-linked guidance** | Patterns span domains (e.g. stress + messy home + no food logged). |
| **Editable** | Rename areas, add checklists, delete what you don't use. |
| **Local-first** | Your machine, your journal, no account. |

## The 18 default life areas

Grouped by domain. All use: **rating (1–10)**, optional **checklist**, **metrics**, and **notes**.

### Financial & vocational

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Money/Freedom** | Budget, savings, debt, investing, generosity, financial clarity, freedom mindset |
| **Career & Vocation** | Work hours, professional growth, networking, alignment with calling, work boundaries |

*Competitor overlap:* Mint/YNAB (money), LinkedIn habit (career) — Integral ties both to burnout and life balance, not just spreadsheets.

---

### Physical

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Body & Presence** | Movement, mobility, sleep, energy, pain, hydration, mindfulness, embodied presence |
| **Burnout Prevention & Energy Management** | Breaks, boundaries, self-care, screen limits, nature, stress, morning energy, recovery |

**Fitness Hub** (separate tab) handles structured programs (CC, OG, Tibetan Rites, etc.) with progression tables. Body & Presence is the daily holistic check-in; fitness sessions add depth.

*Competitor overlap:* Apple Health, Fitbit (passive data) — Integral is intentional reflection, not wearable sync (yet).

---

### Mental & creative

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Creative/Mental Work** | Deep work, ideas, projects, shipping output, focus, creativity |
| **Learning & Intellectual Growth** | Study, skills, languages, instruments, courses, mental clarity, stretch |

*Split:* **Output** (creative work) vs **input/growth** (learning). Content feeds and General Reading are separate consumption areas.

---

### Relational & domestic

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Family/Logistics** | Household time, dependents, errands, systems, connection at home |
| **Relationships & Social Connection** | Friends, partner, social time, belonging, loneliness |
| **Home & Environment** | Tidying, nature, sustainability, space satisfaction, environment vs mood |

*Competitor overlap:* Cozi (family logistics), various social apps — Integral logs *quality* and loneliness, not follower counts.

---

### Inner life

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Search Practice** | Personal inquiry, journaling, direction, sitting with questions |
| **Spiritual Development** | Meditation, prayer, teachings, gratitude, ritual, connection / peace |
| **Emotional Wellbeing** | Emotions, regulation, therapy, mood, processing |
| **Community & Service** | Helping others, volunteering, civic life, contribution, belonging |

*Search Practice* is your custom inquiry lane — distinct from generic spirituality or therapy.

---

### Cultural — active & consumed

| Area | Tracks |
|------|--------|
| **Cultural Life & Heritage** | Language, traditions, festivals, museums, heritage, identity, intentional cultural exploration |
| **Art You Have Consumed** | Film, TV, music, novels, comics, games, theatre, design — aesthetic intake |
| **General Reading** | Nonfiction, textbooks, memoirs, audiobooks — long-form learning text |
| **Content You Have Consumed** | Articles, podcasts, courses, social, news — short-form feeds |
| **What You Have Eaten** | Meals, awareness, nourishment, energy after eating |

*Split:*
- **Active culture** (participating) → Cultural Life & Heritage  
- **Passive art** (novels, film, music) → Art  
- **Reference reading** → General Reading  
- **Feeds** → Content  
- **Fuel** → What You Have Eaten  

See [INPUT_AND_CONSUMPTION_COMPARISON.md](INPUT_AND_CONSUMPTION_COMPARISON.md) for competitor detail on the consumption side.

## How domains connect (guidance examples)

Integral's guidance engine looks **across** areas:

| Signal | Example guidance |
|--------|------------------|
| Body low + no food logged | Fuel and recovery may be linked |
| Mood low + no art | Input gap worth noticing |
| Feeds heavy + no books | Short-form replacing long-form |
| Creative stuck + no learning | Skills/input may need attention |
| Money + career both soft | Vocational and financial strain together |
| Mood low + loneliness rising | Connection may need care |
| Art often, culture never | Consuming vs practicing culture |
| Stress up + home satisfaction down | Environment and burnout |
| Spiritual practice steady, no service | Inner/outer balance |
| Burnout warning | Protect recovery before pushing output |

## What Integral is not trying to be

| Dedicated tool | Integral's stance |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Full accounting / tax software | Log actions and clarity; not IRS-grade books |
| Calorie database | Nourishment and energy, not macro tracking |
| Goodreads / Letterboxd | Title in notes; not a public catalog |
| Project management (Asana, etc.) | Life development, not task boards |
| Therapy / medical record | Emotional check-ins; not clinical documentation |
| Wearable dashboard | Intentional logs; optional future sync |

**Goal:** *Part of everything* — enough structure in each domain to see your life honestly, without replacing best-in-class vertical tools.

## Customizing the model

- **Delete** categories you won't use (Category editor).
- **Rename** to match your language (e.g. "Vocation" instead of "Career").
- **Add** areas (Parenting, Recovery, Side Business, etc.).
- **Merge** on upgrade: new default categories and checklist items are added automatically; your edits are kept.
